At HealthUniverse.website, operated by Devoraels Ltd, we are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679. This regulation protects the rights and freedoms of individuals with respect to the processing of personal data.

1. What is GDPR?

GDPR is a European Union regulation that gives individuals greater control over their personal data and imposes strict rules on companies that process this data, even if they are located outside the EU.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal data:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email address)
  • IP address and browsing behavior
  • Preferences or interests based on website activity

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We collect and process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent (e.g., for newsletter subscriptions or cookies)
  • Contractual necessity (e.g., providing a service you've requested)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving site performance and security)

4. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability
  • Object to data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: your@email.com

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics, email marketing platforms) that process data in accordance with GDPR. We ensure these providers are GDPR-compliant through contracts and data processing agreements.
